Few facts about the world we live in.

Earth is the third planet out from the Sun, located at just over 149 million kilometers away. At that distance, it takes slightly over 365 days to make one trip around the Sun. That period is called a year. Like most other planets, Earth experiences four seasons each year. The reasons for the seasons are simple: Earth is tilted 23.5 degrees on its axis. As the planet orbits the Sun, different hemispheres get more or fewer amounts of sunlight depending on whether they are tilting toward or away from the Sun.
